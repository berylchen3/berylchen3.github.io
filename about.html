Research & Selected Projects

Reinforcement Learning for Robotic Manipulation

LL4MA Lab, Kahlert School of Computing — Feb. 2025 – Present

Designing and implementing custom Gymnasium environments for the Franka Panda robotic arm in PyBullet, focusing on pick-and-place manipulation. I train Soft Actor-Critic (SAC) policies with dense reward shaping and domain randomization, and I am exploring hierarchical control by separating grasping and placement policies coordinated by a higher-level switching policy.

CHPC Security & User Behavior Analysis

Center for High Performance Computing — May 2024 – Aug. 2024

Assisted in a security study involving CHPC users by designing surveys, cleaning and analyzing user log data, and combining Python scripts with SQL-based aggregation. The goal was to better understand user behaviors and support the design of robust security measures.

Kahlert School of Computing — Aug. 2024 – Dec. 2024
